Universal, a concept rooted in the principles of inclusivity and equality, embodies the idea of providing access and opportunities for all individuals, regardless of their background, abilities, or circumstances. The notion of universality extends across various domains, be it education, healthcare, or social services, aiming to bridge the gaps and create a more equitable society. In education, the pursuit of universal education strives to offer quality learning experiences to every child, irrespective of their socio-economic status or geographical location. By eliminating barriers, such as discrimination and lack of resources, universal education endeavors to nurture a generation empowered with knowledge and skills. In healthcare, the vision of universal healthcare is centered around the provision of essential medical services to all citizens without financial burden. This approach seeks to ensure that everyone can access healthcare facilities and lead healthier lives.
